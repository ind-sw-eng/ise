## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Contact Questions

**Q: Who should I contact for urgent issues?**
A: For academic emergencies, contact Paolo Tell directly. For technical issues, use the course forum first, then email if needed.

**Q: How do I schedule a meeting outside office hours?**
A: Email the relevant staff member with your availability. Include the purpose of the meeting and preferred duration.

**Q: Can I contact professors directly about project ideas?**
A: Yes, but please first discuss with your team and Scrum Master. For industry partnerships, contact Stefano Vincenti.

### Technical Support

**Q: Where do I get help with technical issues?**
A: Use the course forum first. TAs and fellow students often provide quick solutions. For complex issues, attend technical support office hours.

**Q: How do I report a bug or issue with course systems?**
A: Email the technical details to the course staff or post in the dedicated technical support forum.
